Silicon Valley Private AI Forum (K-PAI) is a community of professionals
dedicated to advancing privacy-preserving Artificial Intelligence (AI) technologies and practices.
Our members focus on developing and implementing AI systems that protect data privacy, ensure confidentiality,
and maintain security while delivering powerful capabilities.
Through knowledge sharing, networking, and collaborative innovation,
we explore techniques like federated learning, homomorphic encryption,
and differential privacy to build AI systems that respect individual privacy rights while driving technological progress.
Our community brings together experts from leading Silicon Valley organizations to shape the future of responsible, privacy-first AI development.

---

## Our Vision

A world where artificial intelligence and human agency create harmonious counterpoint — each maintaining their essential characteristics while together composing a future that amplifies human dignity, protects individual autonomy, and unlocks unprecedented innovation.

## Our Mission

K-PAI fosters a pioneering community of professionals dedicated to developing AI solutions through cross-disciplinary collaboration, bridging technical innovation with legal frameworks and humanistic principles to create AI systems that serve humanity while protecting individual privacy.

## Our Expertise

K-PAI's expertise spans virtually every AI-related domain including biotechnology, healthcare, medical research, industrial applications, data centers, cloud infrastructure, storage solutions, mobile technologies, customer service platforms, multi-agent systems, RAG implementations, vector databases, and agentic AI frameworks.

## Strategic Partnerships

We have established a perpetual partnership with KOTRA Silicon Valley as a form of Strategic Alliance. Within 2026, K-PAI will co-host forums with K-BioX, KOTRA Silicon Valley, the Consulate General of Republic of Korea in San Francisco, and the Korean American Bar Association of Northern California (KABANC).
